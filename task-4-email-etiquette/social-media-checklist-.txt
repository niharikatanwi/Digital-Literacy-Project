SOCIAL MEDIA CHECKLIST


Do’s (Responsible Practices)

Think critically before posting and consider the long-term impact of your content
Maintain a respectful and professional tone in all interactions
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
Utilize strong privacy settings to control who can view your content
Be aware of your digital footprint and its implications on your reputation
Give proper credit when sharing someone else’s work or ideas
Engage in constructive and meaningful discussions
Maintain a proper balance of personal and professional content
Regularly review and update your profile for accuracy
Report inappropriate content when necessary
Utilize strong passwords and two-factor authentication
Be culturally sensitive and respectful towards diverse perspectives.


2.Don’ts (Irresponsible Practices)

Don’t share personal information such as your address, phone numbers, or passwords
Don’t share content that is offensive, abusive, discriminatory
Don’t engage in cyberbullying, trolling, online harassment
Don’t spread unverified news, rumors, fake news
Don’t share your personal life information that might compromise your privacy
Don’t act impulsively in an emotional state of mind
Don’t plagiarize content; don’t use other people’s content without permission
Don’t click on suspicious links; don’t download suspicious content
Don’t engage in arguments that might lead to conflict
Don’t portray a negative image online; don’t portray an unprofessional image online
Don’t ignore online site rules; don’t ignore online site standards
Don’t overuse social media in such a way that it affects your productivity.
