SATY SAFE ONLINE CHECKLIST 

Use Strong & Unique Passwords
Create passwords using letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using the same password across apps.

2. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
Always turn on OTP or authentication apps for extra account security.

3.Avoid Clicking on Unknown Links
Do not click suspicious links in emails, messages, or social media—they may be phishing scams.

4.Keep Devices & Apps Updated
Regular updates fix security issues and protect against hackers.

5.Never Share Personal or Banking Details
Do not share passwords, OTPs, ATM PINs, or bank details with anyone online.

6.Be Careful with UPI Payment Requests
On apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, and Paytm, accepting a request means you are paying money—not receiving. Always verify before approving.

7.Never Share OTP or UPI PIN
No bank or official will ever ask for it. Sharing these can lead to instant fraud.

8.Report Cyber Fraud Immediately
If you face any scam, report it on the National Cyber Crime Portal or call Helpline 1930 for quick action and possible recovery of money.
